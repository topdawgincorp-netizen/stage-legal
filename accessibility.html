Our commitment

In plain English: We want STĀGE to work for people with disabilities.

[LEGAL_ENTITY_LEGAL_NAME] is committed to making STĀGE and its related web and mobile experiences accessible to people with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA where applicable, and we work to improve the accessibility of our native apps consistent with platform guidance from Apple and Google.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. Some screens, third-party components, or legacy flows may not yet meet every standard.

Feedback and accommodation requests

In plain English: Tell us what you need — we will try to help.

If you experience difficulty using any part of STĀGE, or need information in an alternative format, contact us at [CONTACT_EMAIL]. Please include:

  • A description of the barrier or requested accommodation
  • The platform (iOS, Android, web) and app version, if known

Third-party content

In plain English: We don’t control everything on the screen.

The Services may display User Content or embed third-party players, payment flows, or maps. We do not control the accessibility of third-party content or SDKs; we encourage Users and partners to provide accessible alternatives where possible.
