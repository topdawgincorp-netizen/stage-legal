1. Purpose; related documents

In plain English: Play fair — these rules keep STĀGE safe and creative.

STĀGE is a community for music and creative collaboration. These Guidelines explain what we allow and what we remove or restrict. They work together with our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and DMCA / Copyright Policy.

More detail: We may publish additional acceptable-use documentation inside the app. If there is a conflict on a specific point, the Terms control.

2. Respect and safety

In plain English: No harassment, hate, threats, or exploitation.

  • No harassment, bullying, stalking, or doxxing.
  • No hate speech targeting individuals or groups based on protected characteristics (as defined by applicable law and our enforcement standards).
  • No credible threats of violence.
  • No sexual exploitation of minors and no content that endangers children. We report to authorities where required.
  • No non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII).
  • No self-harm encouragement or instructions for serious harm.

3. Authenticity and deception

In plain English: Don’t impersonate or mislead.

  • No impersonation of individuals, artists, labels, brands, or officials in a misleading way.
  • No fraud, scams, phishing, or deceptive monetization schemes.
  • Disclose paid promotions as required by law and our Terms (FTC-style material connection disclosures).

4. Intellectual property

In plain English: Only post what you have rights to post.

  • Upload only content you own or have permission to use, including samples, covers, beats, artwork, and trademarks.
  • For copyright complaints, see our DMCA Policy.

5. Music-specific expectations

In plain English: Respect artists and rightsholders.

  • No piracy or sharing of stolen leaks intended to circumvent rightsholders’ releases.
  • Label unofficial remixes/remasters clearly where appropriate; you remain responsible for clearances.

6. Spam and manipulation

In plain English: Don’t game the platform.

  • No spam, fake engagement, bot armies, or inauthentic metrics manipulation.
  • No malware, exploits, or attempts to compromise accounts or systems.

7. Illegal activity and regulated goods

In plain English: Follow the law.

  • No use of STĀGE for illegal purposes, including sales of illegal drugs, weapons where prohibited, or other unlawful transactions.
  • Events (including alcohol venues) must comply with applicable laws; organizers and attendees are responsible for age restrictions and venue rules.

8. Marketplace and payments integrity

In plain English: Honor your deals; don’t abuse payments.

  • Accurate listings, honest fulfillment timelines, and fair dispute communication with buyers/sellers.
  • No chargeback abuse, payment fraud, or circumventing platform payment flows where required.

9. Enforcement

In plain English: We may warn, remove content, suspend, or ban.

We may take action including warnings, removing Content, limiting distribution, suspending features, or terminating accounts. Factors include severity, repetition, risk of harm, and legal obligations.

10. Reporting

In plain English: Use in-app reporting or email when available.

Use in-app reporting tools where provided, or contact [CONTACT_EMAIL] with links, screenshots, and a clear description of the issue.
