3. Personal Data we collect

In plain English: Account info, content, usage, device data, and data from partners.

Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect:

3.1 Data you provide

  • Account and profile: name, username, email, phone (if provided), password (hashed), profile photo, bio, links, preferences.
  • User Content: posts, messages, audio/video uploads, comments, metadata (e.g., timestamps).
  • Payments (when you pay): we do not store full payment card numbers on our servers; payment data is processed by Stripe (or another provider). We may receive limited payment metadata (transaction IDs, last four digits, billing country, status).
  • Support communications: emails or in-app messages you send us.
  • Verification (if offered): information you submit for identity or creator verification, which may include government-issued ID where permitted and necessary.

3.2 Data collected automatically

  • Device and app data: device type, OS, app version, identifiers (e.g., advertising IDs if used and permitted), IP address, language, time zone.
  • Usage data: features used, interactions, crash logs, diagnostics, performance metrics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies (web): see our Cookie Policy for browser cookies, pixels, and similar technologies on websites we operate. Native apps use SDKs and device technologies as described in this Policy and Subprocessors.

3.3 Data from third parties

  • Sign-in providers (if enabled): basic profile info from OAuth providers.
  • Payment processors: payment status and fraud signals as permitted.
  • App stores: install/receipt-related signals as provided by Apple/Google.

3.4 Biometric and audio data

We do not use your voice to create a biometric template for authentication unless we launch a specific feature and provide separate notice/consent where required. Audio you upload as User Content is processed to provide the Services (storage, streaming, moderation, safety).

If we introduce biometric processing regulated under Illinois BIPA, Texas law, or elsewhere, we will update this Policy and obtain consent where required.

Audio fingerprinting / Content ID. Unless we expressly disclose otherwise in-product and in this Policy, we do not operate a proprietary audio fingerprinting or “Content ID” system to match your uploads against a reference database for copyright enforcement. If we or a vendor introduce such processing, we will update this Policy and, where required, provide additional notice or obtain consent.
